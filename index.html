ABOUT

Villa Mileti is a renovated, traditional Dalmatian stone house. Originally built in the 1890s, the villa, situated on cascade terrain, includes a private pool, two bedrooms, two bathrooms, Internet access, air-conditioning, and a fully equipped kitchen. The beautiful property spreads across 2000 m² and has total privacy, while boasting an unforgettable view of the Mediterranean Sea. Private parking included.

The old stone house keeps its original spirit; however, modern architecture provides all the amenities of a newer building. Throughout the property you will feel a slight touch of wilderness. The picturesque view of the local islands will leave you feeling far away from civilization.

Ground Floor

The spacious, open-concept ground floor holds the living room, kitchen and dining room. Three glass doors lead to a stone terrace overlooking the pool and ocean. The couch provides additional sleeping space, pulling out into a double sized bed, where two additional guests can sleep comfortably. For those who need to stay connected, there is satellite TV on a LCD screen, and unlimited Wi-Fi accessible Internet. The dining area has a big table, easily accommodating 4 people. The fully equipped kitchen includes a stove, oven, dishwasher, microwave, coffee maker, toaster, fridge and freezer.

First Floor

The first floor is accessible by stairs right from the ground floor and, due to the fact that the house was built on a cascading terrain, from a separate door on the backside of the house. This floor features two bedrooms, each with a relaxing view overlooking the sea. Each bedroom has a queen-sized bed. One bedroom features an en suite bathroom; the second bathroom is accessible from the hallway.

Outside

The cascading terrain provides various levels of greenery and trees to enjoy the smell of salt air and the view over the sea and islands. Do not be surprised if you meet some wild sheep or a horse around the property. The main terrace includes a swimming pool (4m x 8m), sunbeds, and an old fashioned table for breakfast and dinner. Situated on additional terrace levels are benches and a hammock for a relaxing afternoon nap. A large, handmade stone barbeque is the centerpiece of the mid-level. On the uppermost level are more garden benches for relaxation or a bite to eat.

LOCATION

Villa Mileti is situated in a small village called Tribanj Šibuljina right next to Starigrad Paklenica. From Zadar, which is the nearest big city including an airport (Zadar Airport - Zemunik), it is a 45 min drive by car. It is possible to take the lovely country road which was mostly built right by the seashore, or arrive over the highway where you have to take the exit called Maslenica.

Feel free to contact us for any further information you may need.

VISIT PLACES

You don’t have to go far to find great reasons to stay here. There is a wonderful hiking trail that runs along the property and you can become acquainted with the local area at your leisure. The sea is just 5 minutes down the hill by foot. From here, the main road runs alongside the water and can take you to many small beaches in either direction where the water can be enjoyed. Groceries can also be picked up here on the street at the bottom of the hill. Just a 10-minute drive away, in Starigrad, plenty of shops, restaurants, and cafés are here to fill every need. It is also possible to rent a boat, bicycle, or kayak. For those looking for more organized activities, trips to the surrounding islands are a very common activity to be booked here. In Vinjerac, you can find the tavern “Kod Pece”. A top Dalmatian restaurant, they offer a variety of Croatian cuisine, specializing of course in seafood. If there is more information you are looking for in regards to the area or simply about Vila Miletti, don't hesitate to contact us and we will be happy to assist you with all inquiries.
